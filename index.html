About

I'm Julia Anderson, UX Designer

I am a recent UX graduate with a design background and over a decade of experience in marketing within the financial services industry.

Inspired by a desire to help others, I combine strong analytical skills with a user-centered design approach to craft elegant and effective solutions that help companies make an impact.

I genuinely love connecting with people and learning about what makes them tick. In my free time I am a book-obsessed, blue-minded, true-crime enthusiast and culinary adventurer who is always cooking up a new travel itinerary.
